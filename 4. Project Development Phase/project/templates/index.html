About

Our project is focused on a crucial task: predicting bankruptcies in companies. We do this by harnessing the power of computer programs and smart algorithms that learn from financial data. One of the challenges we tackle is dealing with imbalanced data, where there are far fewer bankrupt companies compared to those that are financially stable. Our mission is to provide valuable insights to businesses and investors, enabling them to make well-informed decisions. By leveraging advanced techniques and thorough data analysis, we aim to create accurate and reliable predictions that can significantly impact the way people manage their investments and assess business risks.
